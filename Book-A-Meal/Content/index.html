    About Book-A-Meal

    We are delighted to introduce to you our Company, Book-A-Meal Limited, (BAM or the Company), trading as Book-A-Meal; a fast food restaurant with outlets located in Lekki Phase 1, (on Admiralty Way & Opposite Farm City), Circle Mall-Jakande, Chevron Drive, Ikota, Ikoyi, Festival Mall, Festac, Gbagada & – MMA2 Ikeja all in Lagos.
